Homemade Pizza Recipe

Prep time: 2 hours Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  1. 6 bacon rashers
  2. 150g baby spinach leaves
  3. 1/2 cups tomato pizza sauce
  4. 2 medium pizza bases
  5. 1 cup grated cheese
  6. 100g black olives

Method

Step 1: Preheat oven to 220Â°C. Heat a large frying pan over high heat. Add the bacon and cook, turning occasionally, for 5 minutes or until bacon is crisp. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.

Step 2: Add spinach to pan and cook, stirring, for 2-3 minutes or until wilted. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from heat.

Step 3:Place pizza bases on an oven tray. Spread evenly with sauce. Sprinkle with half the cheese. Top with spinach, bacon and black olives. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and top with tomatoes. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is golden brown. Remove from oven and serve immediately.
